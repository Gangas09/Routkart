About Rout Groceries

Your Trusted Grocery Partner

At Rout Groceries, we believe that everyone deserves access to fresh, high-quality food without the hassle of visiting multiple stores. Our mission is to make grocery shopping convenient, affordable, and enjoyable.

Founded in 2007, we've grown from a small local business to a trusted grocery delivery service serving thousands of satisfied customers. Our team carefully selects each product to ensure it meets our high standards for quality and freshness.

We work directly with local farmers and trusted suppliers to bring you the best products at competitive prices. Our commitment to sustainability means we prioritize eco-friendly packaging and support local communities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to most urban and suburban areas within a 20km radius of our distribution centers. During checkout, you can enter your zip code to confirm if we deliver to your location.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website. Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, choose your delivery time slot, and complete the checkout process.

What is your delivery fee?

We offer free delivery on all orders over 499. Same-day delivery may have an additional charge depending on the time of order.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order up to 2 hours before your scheduled delivery time. After that, our team will have already started preparing your order, so changes may not be possible.

What if I'm not home during delivery?

If you're not home, our delivery driver will leave your groceries in a safe, shaded spot at your doorstep. For perishable items, we use insulated bags to maintain freshness for up to 2 hours.

Do you offer organic products?

Yes! We have a wide selection of certified organic fruits, vegetables, dairy products, and pantry items. You can filter products by "organic" when browsing our categories.

What is your return policy?

If you're not satisfied with any item in your order, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery for a full refund or replacement. We stand behind the quality of all our products.
