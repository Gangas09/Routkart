Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we offer home delivery for all orders above 499. Our delivery service is available within a 20km radius of our store location. Delivery times are typically between 9 AM and 8 PM, and you can choose a 2-hour delivery window that works best for you.

How do I pay for my order?

We accept multiple payment options including UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery (where available).

How can I report a problem with a product I purchased?

If you're not satisfied with any product you've purchased from Rout Groceries, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery. You can call our customer service at +91-7735613751, email us at support@routk5401gmail.com, or use the contact form above. We'll be happy to offer a replacement or refund.

Can I schedule my delivery?

Yes, you can choose a convenient delivery slot while placing your order.
